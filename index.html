Company Introduction 公司介绍

Shanghai Yuqian Trading 上海煜谦贸易有限公司

Yuqian Group is an international trade company mainly engaged in fruit trade. Through 22 years of operation and accumulation, it has laid out a global fruit supply chain, possessing rich international fruit supply chain resources, mature domestic and foreign sales channels and sales experience, as well as professional refrigeration technology and an international logistics and transportation system. 煜谦集团是以水果贸易为主营业务的国际贸易公司,通过22年的经营与积累，布局全球水果供应链，拥有丰富的国际水果供应链资源，成熟的国内外销售渠道和销售经验，以及专业的冷藏技术和国际物流运输体系。

The company's headquarters is in Shanghai, and it also has branch offices in Beijing, Guangzhou, and Jiaxing. The company owns multi-temperature zone constant-temperature cold storage with a total area of 10,000 square meters, with an annual import trade volume of over 1,000 containers. It cooperates with China Eastern Airlines on more than 100 direct charter flights from the United States, Chile, and Peru every year, and occupies an important position in the international fruit trade market. 公司总部在上海，同时在北京、广州、嘉兴均设有分公司。公司拥有总面积达10000㎡的多温区恒温冷库，年进口贸易量达到1000多条柜，与东方航空公司合作美国、智利、秘鲁直航包机100多架次/年，在国际水果贸易市场占有重要地位。

Globally, Yuqian Group has established stable and good trade partnerships with many well-known fruit suppliers, and owns a variety of main products, including cherries, grapes, blueberries, citrus fruits, apples, avocados, stone fruits, durians, young coconuts, pitayas, etc. Its global direct procurement covers North America, South America, Europe, Africa, Asia, and Oceania, forming a colorful fruit world. 在全球，煜谦集团与众多知名的水果供应商建立了稳定、良好的贸易伙伴关系，拥有多款主营产品，包括车厘子、提子、蓝莓、柑橘、苹果、牛油果、核果、榴莲、椰青、火龙果等，全球直采覆盖北美洲、南美洲、欧洲、非洲、亚洲、大洋洲，构成了一幅丰富多彩的水果世界。

In China, Yuqian has set up self-operated stalls in 10 imported fruit wholesale markets across the country. It not only has an experienced and large sales team, but also has a strong distribution network covering key cities such as Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Qingdao, Wuhan, Changsha, Chengdu, and Xi'an. It has established win-win cooperative relationships with many well-known domestic online and offline supermarkets, including Sam's Club, Hema Fresh, Hema NB, Metro, Meituan, JD.com, Walmart, Pagoda, Yonghui, and Dingdong Maicai. 在国内，煜谦在全国各地10个进口水果批发市场均设立了自营档口，不仅拥有经验丰富、庞大的销售团队，还拥有覆盖北京，上海，广州，深圳，青岛，武汉，长沙，成都、西安等重点城市的强大分销渠道，与山姆、盒马、盒马NB、麦德龙、美团、京东、沃尔玛、百果园、永辉、叮咚买菜等多家国内知名线上线下商超建立合作共赢关系。

Yuqian Group has laid out a global fruit trade through a complete set of marketing solutions from direct sourcing at the source to global sales. It covers fruit trade, supply chain services, variety research and development, planting, preservation, and deep processing, connecting the entire chain of fruits from a single seed to consumers' dining tables. 煜谦集团通过从源头直采到销售全球的整套营销解决方案，布局全球水果贸易，从水果贸易、供应链服务，到品种研发与种植、保鲜、深加工，打通水果从一颗种子到消费者餐桌的全链路。

At present, Yuqian Agricultural Technology strictly controls the source producing areas through various forms such as self-construction, cooperation, and shareholding in high-quality producing areas across the country, including Xinjiang, Yunnan, Shaanxi, and Anhui. It leads fruit cultivation, creates value for growers, and delivers beauty to consumers. 目前煜谦农业科技在全国优质产区新疆、云南、陕西、安徽等地通过自建、合作、参股等多种形式严格把控源头产地，引领水果种植，为种植户创造价值，为消费者业传递美好。

In the future, relying on 22 years of industry experience to connect and drive farmers, we will expand businesses in variety research and development, planting, and deep processing. We will truly become a full-link fruit industry expert from the branch to the dining table, and strive to be the most professional global fruit service provider. 未来，依托22年的行业经验联农带农，拓展品种研发、种植和深加工业务，真正成为从枝头到餐桌的全链路果业专家，并致力于成为最专业的全球水果服务商。

Yuqian is committed to moving forward steadily on the path to becoming a world-class fruit agricultural technology enterprise. 煜谦，在成为全球卓越的水果农业科技企业的道路上，笃行致远。

ORIGIN CENTER 产地中心

Adhering to the industrial philosophy of "high quality, high safety and high value", Riverking Origin Center has currently established "six plantation and one center", including the blueberry industrial plantation of Yunnan Province, the cherry industrial plantation of Shaanxi Province, the Anhui International Patent Grape Industrial plantation, the Anhui Apricot and Plum Industrial plantation, the Xinjiang prune plantation, the Chilean cherry plantation and the China International Patent Grape Industrial Chain Center. 煜谦产地中心秉承 “高品质、高安全、高价值” 的产业理念，目前构建了 “六个基地和一个中心”，分别是云南省蓝莓产业基地、陕西省车厘子产业基地、安徽国际专利葡提产业基地、安徽杏李类产业基地、新疆核果基地、智利车厘子基地和中国国际专利葡提全产业链中心。

Chile's NATURE SOUTH cherry packaging plant is located in Curicó, the sixth cherry-producing region of Chile. It covers an area of 18 hectares with a building area of 32 hectares, has 24 packaging lines, and packages 9,000 tons of cherries annually. 中国国际专利葡提全产业链中心：智利车厘子基地、陕西车厘子基地、安徽国际专利葡提产业基地、安徽杏李产业基地、云南蓝莓基地、新疆核果基地。

China International Patent Grape Whole Industry Chain Center: Chilean Cherry Base, Shaanxi Cherry Base, Anhui International Patent Grape Industry Base, Anhui Apricot-Plum Industry Base, Yunnan Blueberry Base, Xinjiang Stone Fruit Base. 在全国优质产区新疆、云南、陕西、安徽等地，通过自建、合作、参股等多种形式严格把控源头产地。

Sales Center 销售中心

WHOLESALE BUSINESS 批发业务

Focusing on self-operated stalls in China's tier-1 and tier-2 wholesale markets, regional wholesale market distributors, retail chain supply chain enterprises, and corporate and institutional group purchase customers, we provide stable and efficient fruit supply and logistics solutions. 专注于国内一、二级大型批发市场自营档口、区域批发市场分销商、零售连锁供应链企业及企业事业团购客户，提供稳定、高效的水果供应与物流解决方案。

OVERSEAS SALES BUSINESS 海外销售业务

Riverking has established a professional overseas sales team, closely cooperating with fruit production areas and brands all over the world, and has a national terminal retail. Relying on the advantage of supply sources, it has achieved a best-selling sales chain of global buying and global selling. At present, it has offices in Hong Kong, Peru, Chile, Australia, Thailand and North America. 煜谦设立专业的销售团队，与遍布全球的水果产区及品牌紧密合作，并拥有全国终端零售。凭借资源优势，实现全球买全球卖的畅销销售链路。目前在香港、秘鲁、智利、澳洲、泰国及北美地区均设有办事处。

KA CHANNEL OPERATION BUSINESS KA 渠道运营业务

Relying on Riverking’s global fruit supply chain resources (including production areas such as Southeast Asia, South America, North America, Australia and New Zealand) and the national distribution network, we are committed to becoming an efficient, agile and low-waste expert in full-channel retail services. Currently, the cooperation channels include large supermarkets, fresh e-commerce, community group buying, chain fruit stores and so on. Nine regional distribution centers enable 24-hour replenishment, establishing a leading fresh-fruit supply network. 依托公司全球水果供应链资源（覆盖东南亚、南美、北美、澳新等产区）和全国分销网络，致力成为高效、敏捷、低损耗的全渠道零售服务专家。服务覆盖全国重要省份，合作渠道包括大型商超、生鲜电商、社区团购、连锁水果店等。全域仓配中心支撑 24 小时极速补货，打造行业领先的鲜果供应网络。

DIRECT SELLING BUSINESS 直销业务

We are committed to building an efficient, transparent, and low-loss direct-selling platform that directly connects the company's core products with downstream buyers, such as second and third-tier wholesale vendors, chain fruit stores, supply chain service providers, and corporate&institutional group-purchasing clients. By cutting out intermediate layers and optimizing the supply chain, we are able to ensure fresher produce and lower distribution costs. 致力于打造一个高效、透明、低损耗的直销平台，直接连接公司核心单品与下游采购商（二三级批发档口、连锁水果店、供应链服务商、企业事业团购等），通过减少中间环节，优化供应链，进一步保障水果新鲜度，降低流通成本。

iverking has established sorting warehouses in over 10 cities across the country, covering over 100,000 square meters and processing 350,000 orders per day at peak. 全国 10 余个城市布局分拣仓，总面积超 10 万平米，峰值单量可达 100 万单 / 天。

It offers a mature, full-category fruit warehousing and sorting solution: multi-temperature storage space, precise sorting and customized packaging services, and choosing the best packaging solutions based on the characteristics of the goods. 煜谦拥有成熟的全品类水果全链条仓储分拣解决方案：多温区仓储空间；精准分拣与定制化包装服务，根据货物特性选择最优包装方案。
