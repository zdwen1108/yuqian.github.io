Company Introduction 公司介绍

Shanghai Yuqian Trading 上海煜谦贸易有限公司

Yuqian Group is an international trade company mainly engaged in fruit trade. Through 22 years of operation and accumulation, it has laid out a global fruit supply chain, possessing rich international fruit supply chain resources, mature domestic and foreign sales channels and sales experience, as well as professional refrigeration technology and an international logistics and transportation system. 煜谦集团是以水果贸易为主营业务的国际贸易公司,通过22年的经营与积累，布局全球水果供应链，拥有丰富的国际水果供应链资源，成熟的国内外销售渠道和销售经验，以及专业的冷藏技术和国际物流运输体系。

Globally, Yuqian Group has established stable and good trade partnerships with many well-known fruit suppliers, and owns a variety of main products, including cherries, grapes, blueberries, citrus fruits, apples, avocados, stone fruits, durians, young coconuts, pitayas, etc. Its global direct procurement covers North America, South America, Europe, Africa, Asia, and Oceania, forming a colorful fruit world. 在全球，煜谦集团与众多知名的水果供应商建立了稳定、良好的贸易伙伴关系，拥有多款主营产品，包括车厘子、提子、蓝莓、柑橘、苹果、牛油果、核果、榴莲、椰青、火龙果等，全球直采覆盖北美洲、南美洲、欧洲、非洲、亚洲、大洋洲，构成了一幅丰富多彩的水果世界。

Talents Shine, We're Waiting for You 煜才闪耀 职等你来

Yuqian, starting its journey from Shanghai's Shiliupu in 2003, has developed into a fruit agricultural technology enterprise rooted in China and connected to the world. By building an efficient, transparent, and sustainable global fruit supply chain network, it covers the entire industrial ecological chain "from a seed to the consumer's dining table". Together with global partners, it jointly leads the fruit industry towards a high-quality and sustainable future, and continues to innovate for a better life for humanity. 煜谦自2003年，从上海十六铺启航，已发展成为一家扎根中国、链接全球的水果农业科技企业。通过构建高效、透明、可持续的全球水果供应链网络，覆盖“从一颗种子到消费者餐桌”的完整产业生态链。与全球合作伙伴，共同引领水果产业向高品质、可持续的未来迈进，为人类美好生活而持续创新。
